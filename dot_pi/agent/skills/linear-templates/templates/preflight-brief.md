# Pre-flight Brief Template

Posted as a Linear project update before starting implementation. Voice: agent to lead engineer. Must be skimmable in under 30 seconds.

```markdown
## 🤖 Pre-flight Brief: [task name]
**Scope:** [SUR-XXX, ...] | **Branch:** `[branch name]`

### Goal (confirm my understanding)
[1 sentence — what I believe this change achieves]

### What I Will Change
| Component | Change | Why |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| `path/to/file` | [what changes] | [reason] |

### Choices I Made
- **[Choice made]** over [alternative] — [rationale in ≤10 words]

### Assumptions
- [Assumption] — [what I did because the requirement was silent on this]

### Risks & How I Am Handling Them
- [Risk]: [mitigation]

### Out of Scope
- [What I am not touching and why]
```
